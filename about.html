Welcome to the Digital Token Notifier Project
 The Digital Token Notifier prototype revolutionizes queue management in various settings, from bustling banks to busy canteens. Say goodbye to outdated manual systems and welcome efficiency through technology.
How It Works:
 Seamless Integration: The Digital Token Notifier seamlessly integrates hardware components including the ESP8266, buzzer, and vibrator with smart notifications.
Real-Time Alerts: When a customer's token matches the current serving number, the system springs into action.
Audible Alerts: The buzzer emits a sharp and attention-grabbing sound, ensuring that customers are promptly notified of their turn.
Silent Notifications: For added discretion, the vibrator provides a silent notification to the token holder. This feature is especially beneficial in environments where noise must be kept to a minimum.
