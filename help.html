Getting Started

Steps to use "Digital Token Notifier"

1.Click on the start button:

Locate the "start" icon on Digital Token Notifier Application.

2.Login with Username and Password:

After starting the application,you'll likely to promoted to log in.Enter your username and password credential into the designated field.

3.Choose your service:

Once logged in,you'll need to select the specific service which you want to give notification.

4.Ready to use Token in your service:

After selecting your desired service, you're now set to utilize the Digital Token Notifier in your work, depending on its functionality.

5.Give feedback for better work:

Feedback is crucial for any application.If you encounter any issues,have suggetions for improvement, or simply want to provide general feedback.
